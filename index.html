The Spirit of Clyde & Conrad

At Clyde & Conrad Honey Co., we believe that truly exceptional honey is a collaboration between the beekeeper and the natural world. Our journey is centered in the heart of Adderbury, where the rolling landscapes provide a lush sanctuary for our bees.

We reject the fast-paced nature of modern food production. Instead, we embrace a "slow honey" philosophy—taking only what the bees can spare and bottling it by hand in tiny, precious batches. This artisanal approach ensures that the unique "terroir" of the local Oxfordshire countryside is captured in every single jar we produce.

Why We Choose Raw & Unprocessed

Most commercial honey is a shadow of its true self. To keep it liquid forever, it is pasteurized at high heat and ultra-filtered, a process that strips away the very things that make honey a miracle: the live enzymes, the delicate aromatic oils, and the beneficial local pollen.

Our honey is Raw. This means it never leaves the temperature of the hive. By keeping it raw, we preserve its complex medicinal properties, its rich antioxidants, and its incredible depth of flavor. When you taste our honey, you are tasting the exact nectar the bees gathered—untouched, untainted, and brimming with the vitality of the British countryside.
