The Spirit of the Hive

At Clyde & Conrad Honey Co., we believe that truly exceptional honey is not just a food, but a reflection of the landscape. Our bees spend their days navigating the lush, ancient hedgerows and sun-drenched wildflower meadows of Adderbury, collecting nectar that carries the very essence of North Oxfordshire.

Our process is a slow, quiet art. We work in harmony with the seasons, interfering as little as possible to ensure that the delicate life of the hive is respected. Every jar we produce is a single-origin masterpiece, hand-poured and unique to the specific time and place of its harvest.

The Raw Difference

Most modern honey is over-processed—heated and pressurized until its soul is stripped away. At Clyde & Conrad, we choose a different path. Our honey remains Raw, meaning it is never heated above the natural temperature of the beehive.

This gentle approach preserves the living enzymes, the intricate aromatic compounds, and the precious local pollens that make raw honey a true gift for the body. When you taste our harvest, you aren't just tasting sugar; you are tasting the minerals, the flora, and the vibrant health of the natural world.
