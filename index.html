Pure Organic Honey, Just as Nature Intended

Small-batch, raw honey sustainably harvested from local Adderbury hives — unprocessed, preservative-free, and naturally fresh.

Our Story

Clyde & Conrad Honey Co. is rooted in the belief that exceptional honey begins with healthy bees and respect for the land. Our hives are located in the countryside surrounding Adderbury, where bees forage freely among wildflowers, clover, and native plants.

We practice small-scale, organic beekeeping without chemicals, pesticides, or artificial feeding. Our honey is harvested in limited batches, taking only what the bees can naturally spare.

Each jar is bottled fresh and left unfiltered and unheated to preserve its natural flavour, aroma, and texture — honey exactly as the bees created it.

Why Natural

We believe all-natural honey offers the best flavour and the purest goodness. Free from preservatives, additives, or unnecessary processing, it retains all its natural enzymes, aromas, and nutrients.

Our honey is raw and unprocessed — straight from the hive to your table, rich in flavor and wholesome goodness.
