Small-batch, raw honey sustainably harvested from local Adderbury hives — unprocessed, preservative-free, and naturally fresh.

Our Story

Clyde & Conrad Honey Co. is rooted in the belief that exceptional honey begins with healthy bees and respect for the land. Our hives are located in the countryside surrounding Adderbury, where bees forage freely among wildflowers, clover, and native plants.

Why Natural

Free from preservatives, additives, or unnecessary processing, it retains all its natural enzymes, aromas, and nutrients. Straight from the hive to your table.
