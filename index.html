Pure, Raw Honey from the Adderbury Meadows

Hand-Crafted by Brothers

We are two brothers, fueled by an unwavering passion for the craft of beekeeping and a simple, honest goal: creating fully natural honey that everyone can enjoy straight from the hive. For us, honey is more than a product; it is a labor of love and a commitment to preserving the vibrant health of our local ecosystem.

Every golden drop of Clyde & Conrad honey is produced right here in our corner of the world. We believe in the beauty of the human touch, which is why every single jar is meticulously canned and labeled by hand. This artisanal approach ensures that when you open a jar, you are experiencing the pure, untamed essence of the hive, delivered with the care only a small-scale, family-run harvest can provide.

Preserving the Superfood

Commercial honey is often treated as a commodity, subjected to intense heat and pressure that destroys its living enzymes. At Clyde & Conrad, we refuse to compromise. Our honey is 100% Raw, meaning it stays exactly as the bees intended.

By avoiding pasteurization, we keep the delicate wildflower pollens and antioxidants intact. This is honey at its most potent—a complex, multi-layered superfood that tastes as vibrant as the meadows our bees call home.
