The Spirit of Perry Tree Farm

At Clyde & Conrad Honey Co., we believe that truly exceptional honey is a collaboration between the beekeeper and the natural world. Our journey began at Perry Tree Farm, where the rolling landscapes of Adderbury provide a sanctuary for our bees. Here, amongst the seasonal bloom of hawthorn, horse chestnut, and summer wildflowers, our colonies forage in peace, far from the reach of industrial agriculture.

We reject the fast-paced nature of modern food production. Instead, we embrace a "slow honey" philosophy—taking only what the bees can spare and bottling it by hand in tiny, precious batches. This artisanal approach ensures that the unique "terroir" of Adderbury is captured in every single jar we produce.

Why We Choose Raw & Unprocessed

Most commercial honey is a shadow of its true self. To keep it liquid forever, it is pasteurized at high heat and ultra-filtered, a process that strips away the very things that make honey a miracle: the live enzymes, the delicate aromatic oils, and the beneficial local pollen.

Our honey is Raw. This means it never leaves the temperature of the hive. By keeping it raw, we preserve its complex medicinal properties, its rich antioxidants, and its incredible depth of flavor. When you taste our honey, you are tasting the exact nectar the bees gathered—untouched, untainted, and brimming with the vitality of the British countryside.

The Seasonal Harvest

Our collection represents the changing colors and scents of the Oxfordshire seasons. Because our honey is raw, its texture is a living thing—changing from silky liquid to a luxurious spreadable crystal over time.
