Our Heritage & Craft

Clyde & Conrad Honey Co. began as a passion project fueled by a deep respect for the remarkable honeybee. Nestled in the rolling green landscapes of North Oxfordshire, our hives sit amidst the ancient hedgerows and wildflower meadows of Adderbury.

Unlike commercial honey producers, we embrace a "slow honey" movement. We follow the rhythm of the seasons, allowing our bees to forage naturally on the local nectar of hawthorn, chestnut, and summer wildflowers.
